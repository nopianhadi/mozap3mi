## ðŸŽ¯ Karakteristik Material Design yang Diterapkan

### Cards
- **Background**: Putih bersih (`bg-white`)
- **Border**: Subtle gray (`border border-gray-200`)
- **Shadow**: Bertingkat (`shadow-md` â†’ `hover:shadow-2xl`)
- **Rounded**: Konsisten (`rounded-2xl`)
- **Transition**: Smooth (`transition-all duration-300`)

### Icons
- **Background**: Warna solid dengan gradient untuk section berwarna
- **Shape**: Rounded (`rounded-xl` atau `rounded-2xl`)
- **Shadow**: `shadow-lg` untuk depth
- **Colors**: Blue, Indigo, Purple, Green (Material Design palette)

### Typography
- **Headings**: `text-gray-900` (hampir hitam)
- **Body**: `text-gray-600` (medium gray)
- **Muted**: `text-gray-400` (light gray)

### Buttons
- **Primary**: `bg-blue-600 hover:bg-blue-700`
- **Shadow**: `shadow-md hover:shadow-lg`
- **Rounded**: `rounded-lg`
- **Transition**: `transition-all duration-200`

### Color Palette
- **Primary Blue**: `blue-600` (Royal Blue)
- **Secondary**: `indigo-600`, `purple-600`
- **Success**: `green-600`
- **Warning**: `orange-600`
- **Background**: `white`, `gray-50`, `gray-900`
- **Text**: `gray-900`, `gray-600`, `gray-400`
